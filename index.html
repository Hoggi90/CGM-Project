The Idea

As a parent of a child with type 1 diabetes, I want to create a system that allows me to easily monitor my son’s glucose levels, whether I'm with him or away. My goal is to display his continuous glucose monitor (CGM) readings on a large screen, positioned for easy viewing, without constantly checking his phone. This solution will make it much more convenient to keep track of his levels in real-time and ensure he stays safe and healthy.
